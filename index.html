About Our School

Excellence in Education Since 1995

GS Mukura is a leading educational institution offering both Primary and Secondary education. We serve students from P1 to Form 6, providing a comprehensive curriculum that meets national educational standards while fostering individual growth.

Our institution is recognized for its commitment to academic excellence, character development, and community engagement. We believe in educating the whole child - mind, body, and spirit.

  • Established: 1995
  • Primary Students: 850+
  • Secondary Students: 350+
  • Teachers: 65+ qualified staff
  • Facilities: Modern classrooms, science labs, library, computer labs, sports facilities

Our Schools

Primary School (P1-P6)

Our Primary School provides a strong foundation for lifelong learning. We focus on developing core literacy and numeracy skills while nurturing creativity and social development.

  • Ages 6-12 years
  • Comprehensive primary curriculum
  • Extracurricular activities

Secondary School (Form 1-6)

Our Secondary School offers advanced academic programs with specialized streams in Sciences, Arts, Business, and Technical studies. We prepare students for national examinations and higher education.

  • Ages 13-19 years
  • Science, Arts, Business & Technical streams
  • Advanced science laboratories
  • University preparation programs

Admissions Process

Primary School Admissions

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous reports)
  3. Attend an assessment interview
  4. Receive admission decision within 2 weeks
  5. Complete enrollment and payment process

Primary Term Dates 2024

  • Term 1: January 15 - April 5
  • Term 2: May 6 - August 2
  • Term 3: September 2 - November 29

Primary School Hours

Monday - Friday: 7:30 AM - 3:00 PM

Extra-curricular: 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM

Secondary School Admissions

  1. Complete the secondary school application form
  2. Submit P6 leaving certificate and reports
  3. Take entrance examination in Mathematics and English
  4. Interview with Head of Secondary
  5. Receive admission decision within 3 weeks
  6. Select stream (Sciences, Arts, Business, Technical)

Secondary Term Dates 2024

  • Term 1: January 8 - April 12
  • Term 2: May 6 - August 9
  • Term 3: September 2 - December 6

Secondary School Hours

Monday - Friday: 7:30 AM - 4:00 PM

Study Sessions: 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM (Optional)

Weekend Prep: Saturday 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Form 4-6)
